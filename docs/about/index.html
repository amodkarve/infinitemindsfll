About Our Team

Meet the Infinite Minds FLL Team

Who We Are

We are team Infinite Minds - a passionate team of eight middle school students dedicated to exploring robotics and STEM through the FIRST LEGO League (FLL) competitions. FLL, hosted by the non-profit organization FIRST, challenges young minds each year with exciting prompts that inspire innovation and teamwork.

Our Journey

Over the past four years, we've tackled challenges in fields like ocean exploration, energy, logistics, and the arts—learning from incredible professionals, teachers, and experts along the way. These experiences have shaped us not only as problem-solvers but as future innovators and leaders.

What We Want To Do Next

As we wrap up our middle school journey with FLL, we're determined to leave a meaningful legacy by giving back to the community that helped us grow. We want to inspire the next generation of young scientists and innovators while contributing to important causes that align with our values and experiences in FLL.
